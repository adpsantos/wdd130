Overview

Purpose

MealMachine is dedicated to simplifying meal planning and cooking by seamlessly integrating shopping lists, pantry management, and personalized recipe suggestions. Our aim is to reduce food waste, streamline grocery shopping, and ignite culinary creativity, ultimately making every cooking experience enjoyable and efficient.

Audience

My target audience starts from teens to any capable adults who are capable of cooking. People who likes to meal plan, cook or typically goes grocery shopping would enjoy this website.
